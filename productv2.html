Zurich Pressure Guidewire
System Model 100™

First ever

All-in-One

FFR system

No more capital equipment

The innovative Zurich Pressure Guidewire System Model 100™ is
FDA cleared and CE certified device

The world's only All-in-One single-use Fractional Flow Reverse
(FFR) system, consisting of a 0.014" diameter pressure guidewire
and a portable display unit with a custom designed micropro-
cessor. This system eliminates the need to buy and maintain ex-
pensive capital equipment.

Atraumatic and Shapeable Tip

The shapeable radiopaque distal tip
helps the user to safely and confidently
navigate through anatomy and lesions.

Advanced MEMS Sensor

The state-of-the-art MEMS piezoresistive
sensor has exceptional pressure
sensitivity and accurate linear operation
under broad physiologic pressures.

Quick and Easy Wire Connection

Ergonomic handle design with one
push attach / detach of guidewire.

Single Button Operation

Single button function control,
simplifies unit operation.
